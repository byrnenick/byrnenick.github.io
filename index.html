Nick Byrne is a public interest technologist with a focus on digital identity, internet governance, entrepreneurship and democracy.

After launching Digital iD with Australia Post in 2016, Nick launched TypeHuman out of a concern for how technology was disrupting democracy and contributing to growing wealth inequality. Since 2017 he has lead TypeHuman as the founding Managing Director.

Prior to this Nick has over 10 years experience working across corporate Australia, startups, and the not-for-profit world.
