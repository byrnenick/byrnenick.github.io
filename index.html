Nick Byrne is an adventurer and technologist. His goal is to inspire people to pursue ambitious goals for themselves and society.

After launching Digital iD with Australia Post in 2016, Nick launched TypeHuman where we work with leaders who are navigating technological change and its implications for people, society, and the next economy.

Prior to this Nick has over 10 years experience working across corporate Australia, startups, and the not-for-profit world.
